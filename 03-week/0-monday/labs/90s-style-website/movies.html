Horror

Horror films are my favorite category of film. There is just something about the way really good horror films are made that keep my interest peaked. Crafting scary situations without using cheap jump-scares takes a lot of talent and it's something I am able to appreciate. The good thing about horror films is that the bad ones can be entertaining as well. They provide a lot of laughter, so even if the movie itself is bad it can create an entertaining atmosphere as you critique how bad the movie is especially with family and friends.
