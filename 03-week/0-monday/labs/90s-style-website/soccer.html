Favorite Sport

Soccer has played an important role in my life ever since I began playing in Middle-School. When living in Angola I was constantly playing and watching games in my spare time. Playing for both my school and at club level I have always enjoyed the competitiveness of the sport and played as often as I could. I now play as an attacking forward for the University of Houston and continue to love the sport as I have for much of my life!
